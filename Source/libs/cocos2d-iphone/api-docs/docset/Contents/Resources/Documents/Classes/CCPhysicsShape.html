surfaceVelocity

Velocity of the surface of the shape relative to its normal velocity. This is useful for modeling conveyor belts or the feet of a player character. The calculated surface velocity of two colliding shapes by default only affects their friction. The calculated value can be overriden in a CCCollisionPairDelegate pre-solve method. Defaults to CGPointZero.

@property (nonatomic, assign) CGPoint surfaceVelocity
